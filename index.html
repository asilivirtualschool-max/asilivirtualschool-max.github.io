Who We Are

An educational consultancy built for the AI era

We work with public and private schools in Abu Dhabi, Dubai and Sharjah, training teachers and school leaders to use AI tools to design worthwhile, differentiated learning tasks — tiered for every ability level, scaffolded for Students of Determination, and built with success criteria and rubrics that make assessment integral rather than an afterthought.

Every programme is aligned to the frameworks schools are actually judged under, so the work teachers produce doubles as inspection evidence. Alongside teacher CPD, we run introductory AI courses for senior students, preparing them to use these tools well for study, projects and their careers.

Our one non-negotiable principle

The teacher's judgement is the product — AI only drafts.

We train educators to direct, critique and quality-assure AI output — never to hand it to students unchecked. No student data ever enters a prompt, and AI never grades.

Our Approach

Five features of a worthwhile task

Everything we train centres on task quality — because great teaching starts with great tasks. Every task built in our programmes is:

1

Purposeful

Students can say what they are learning and why it matters.

2

Appropriately challenging

Every learner must think hard — routes in, room to stretch.

3

Real-world connected

Authentic contexts from the UAE and students' own lives.

4

Thinking-demanding

Reasoning, justification and creation — not recall alone.

5

Assessable

Clear success criteria producing evidence teachers can judge.

+

Quality-assured

Every AI-assisted resource passes a five-point QA check before it reaches a student.

Inspection-Aligned

Built around the frameworks your school is judged under

Abu Dhabi public and private schools: task design mapped to the indicators ADEK inspectors use, from students' achievement to leadership and self-evaluation.

UAE SIF — DSIB / KHDA

Dubai private schools: evidence-rich tasks that demonstrate teaching for effective learning, assessment and inclusion at inspection.

UAE SIF — SPEA

Sharjah private schools: the same rigorous method, in the language SPEA inspectors expect to see in books and classrooms.

Our responsible-use red lines

  • Teacher in the loop — no AI output reaches a student unchecked.
  • No student data — nothing identifiable ever enters a prompt.
  • AI never grades — feedback is always verified and personalised by the teacher.
  • Transparency — AI assistance is acknowledged and codified in school policy.
